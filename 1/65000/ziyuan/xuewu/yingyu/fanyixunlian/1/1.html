A chill of horror suddenly swept over him.

But finally my curiosity got the best of me.I creep out of the house and followed him on his journey.

4. 天空越来越黑，光线越来越暗

5. 然后传来轻柔的声音，”母亲节快乐！“
Then emerged a tender voice, "Happy Mother's Day."
emerge vi.出现，发生

6[续写：动作描写] 微风轻抚着我的脸颊，我再也不觉得疲劳了
With the breeze stroking my chairs softly, I didn't feel fatigue anymore.
fatigue n.疲惫

6.1[续写：动作描写] 他太累了，就靠在火炉旁，迷迷糊糊的睡着了
Consumed with fatigue, he sat close to the stove and soon dozed off.

7. 一种忧郁和绝望的气氛笼罩着我们
There was an air of gloom and despair hanging（=enveloping=blanketing） over us
gloomy adi.昏暗的，令人沮丧的

8.[续写：情绪描写] 她激动地双颊通红，脸上绽出灿烂的笑容
Her cheeks glowed with excitement and her face broke into a wide,beaming smile.
glow n.光亮，喜悦=glitter=glimmer=glisten

8.1[续写：环境描写] 桔红色的夕阳辉映着群山
The setting sun cast an orange glow over the mountains.

Feel gripped to me as I began to imagine all sorts of things that would happen.
